# Mold Specifications — Glytch: Nemo Edition

Two PVC injection molds: **scalp cap** and **face plate**. Tongue-and-groove
fit at the equator, hidden under the fur collar.

> These are our initial design targets. We welcome factory expert
> recommendations on any spec — material choice, tolerances, surface
> finish, etc. Please suggest improvements based on production experience.

## Target specifications

| Feature | Specification |
|---|---|
| Cap socket holes | Qty: 4 · Ø 4.5 mm ±0.1 mm · depth 4 mm |
| Antenna peg | Ø 4.3 mm · interference fit |
| Wall thickness | 2.5 mm ±0.3 mm |
| Eyes | 3D molded · 5 mm relief · matte black |
| Surface finish | Matte (SPI B-2) |
| Parting line | Hidden at cap-to-fur joint |
| Gate location | Inside cap or rear of face |
| Material | ABS or PVC — TBD with factory |

## Factory-input recommendations we're soliciting

- Recommended material (ABS vs PVC vs other) and why
- Optimal wall thickness for this size/shape
- Best approach for 3D molded eyes (relief vs insert vs paint)
- Antenna peg attachment method (interference fit vs snap vs screw)
- Surface finish options and durability trade-offs
- Suggested draft angles for clean release
- Color matching process and Pantone reference handling
- Recommended MOQ and lead time for initial production run

When the factory returns DFM feedback, this document gets updated in place —
followed by a build-log entry at [laclawclaw.com/build](https://laclawclaw.com/build).
